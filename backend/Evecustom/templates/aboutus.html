Why we built this?


  • Multi event hosting

    • To manage various events simultaneously which becomes complex and unorganised without proper system.

  • To decrease manual labour

    • Registration through manual entries, google forms, spreadsheet can lead to data duplication, errors in entering participants details and difficulty in filtering and sorting.

  • Lack of team management

    • Events which are managed through whatsapp groups. manual task allocation and no proper role assignment can lead to confusion in responsibilities, lack of accountibility and miscommunication.

  • Lack of real time tracking & automated notification


    • As organisers cannot track registration count live, team performnce, track payment and event status and everything requires manual checking and participants do not receive instant mails, reminders, payment conformation updates.

What problem does it solve?


  • Real time analytics dashboard

    • Does the live tracking of registered teams and attendance.

  • Team creation & management

    • Allows the seamless team formation, approval and co-ordinations.

  • Centralised event hosting

    • Provides one unified platform to create create, manage and monitor all events.

  • Role based access control

    • Provides a seperate dashboard for organisers and participants.

  • Automated notification system

    • Gives instant configration reminders and updates.

  • QR based entry pass

    • Provides secure and fast event check-in with unique QR passes.
